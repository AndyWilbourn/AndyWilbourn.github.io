Routes

Squirrel Hill Loop

Length: 10 miles

Effort: Enthusiast

Type: Loop

Slope Score: 7 out of 10




This route puts the ‘hill’ in Squirrel Hill. You’ll be taken around the local parks and through local neighborhoods, but you might be hard pressed to notice as you put your feet down on the pedals!


Allegheny River Trail

Length: 23 miles

Effort: Enthusiast

Type: Loop

Slope Score: 5 out of 10




This casual ride along the river will take you past some of the most outstanding examples of the architecture of the city, taking you right to Point Park and back to the 31st St Bridge.


North and Back

Length: 32 miles

Effort: Roadie

Type: Out and Back

Slope Score: 9 out of 10




Not for the faint of heart, the Northern Out and Back will take you north of the city through the McCandless Township, up into some of the beautiful hills and mountains that surround the city. Full of wonderful sights that you have to earn!
